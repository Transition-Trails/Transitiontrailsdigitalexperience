## â“ FAQ

**Q: Why aren't my search results showing up?**
A: Check your filters - category or status filters might be too restrictive.

**Q: How do I clear all filters?**
A: Click the "Clear All Filters" button at the bottom of the filter panel.

**Q: Can I search by multiple tags?**
A: Yes! Select multiple tags. Components must match ALL selected tags.

**Q: What if I can't find a component?**
A: Try broader search terms or check if filters are active.

**Q: How do I know if a component is documented?**
A: Look for the green "Documented" badge in results, or filter by status.
